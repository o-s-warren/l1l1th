You would have found these art cards pinned to surfaces in Elle's flat, carried in bags, and towering in stacks on the desk and on the floor to be shuffled through whenever looking for inspiration and sustenance. They were obsessed with the way that these small cards, gathered from various places, worked in an indexical fashion: gesturing towards things larger while themselves small enough to keep beside you and, as importantly, beside each other. The next page, which is the final page, attempts to recreate something like the experience of browsing these physical objects - themselves attempting to approximate the vastness of the works they imitate even if only in a small way. This site has attempted to do something similar. Thank you for everything.
